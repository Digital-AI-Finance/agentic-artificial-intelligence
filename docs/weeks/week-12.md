## Final Project Presentations

Students present their research projects on agentic AI topics.

## Exercise

Write a research proposal for an open problem in agentic AI:
1. Identify a meaningful problem
2. Review related work
3. Propose a novel approach
4. Design evaluation methodology

## Discussion Questions

1. What are the most important open problems?
2. How do we ensure agent safety at scale?
3. What role will agents play in 5 years?
