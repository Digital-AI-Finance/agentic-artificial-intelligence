## Exercise

Build a domain knowledge graph system:
1. Extract entities and relationships from documents
2. Construct a queryable knowledge graph
3. Implement multi-hop reasoning
4. Compare with vector-based retrieval

## Discussion Questions

1. When is graph-based retrieval better than vector search?
2. How do you handle entity disambiguation?
3. What are the scaling challenges of knowledge graphs?
